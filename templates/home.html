Doppler Effect Demonstration

Math and Physics Art-Integration Project

About

The purpose of this web-application is to better understand the concepts of the Doppler effect, primarily; red-shifting and blue-shifting, and comes under Electromagnetic Waves. It achieves it's purpose by taking user-desired images and visualizing how they would appear if they were moving towards or away from the observer at a high velocity. The back-end of the application also uses the mathematical concept of Matricies, as it deals with manipulation of images using digital color-spaces which is similar to multidimenssional calculations.
